    <!-- Introduction Section -->
    <div id="introduction">
        <p>Marlboro, a globally recognized cigarette brand, has etched its name into the world of motorsports, especially in Formula 1. Since the 1970s, Marlboro has been a prominent sponsor of some of the most successful teams and drivers, including Ferrari and McLaren. The iconic red and white Marlboro livery became synonymous with speed, competition, and the high-octane world of racing.</p>
        <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marlboro_(cigarette)" target="_blank">Learn more about Marlboro</a>

        <h2>Features of Marlboro's Racing Presence</h2>
        <p>Marlboro's involvement in motorsports is not just about sponsorship; it's about branding and creating a lasting visual impact. The company’s iconic red and white colors became a defining feature of racing liveries, seen on the cars of champions like Ayrton Senna and Michael Schumacher.</p>
